Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BharatPlate HSRP ("we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use this website and place an order.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and delivery address
  • Your vehicle registration number (as entered by you)
  • Payment confirmation details from our payment processor, Razorpay (we do not receive or store your card, UPI PIN, or net banking credentials)
  • Basic usage and interaction data via Meta Pixel, used for ad performance measurement (see Section 4)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your HSRP order
  • To contact you regarding order status, delivery, or issues
  • To improve our website and customer experience
  • To measure and optimise our advertising campaigns

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. Your payment information is handled directly by Razorpay under its own privacy and security policies; we never store your full card or banking details on our servers.

4. Meta Pixel & Advertising

We use Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram) to understand how visitors interact with this site and to measure the effectiveness of our ads. This may involve Meta setting cookies in your browser. You can control ad personalisation through your Meta account's ad settings, or by using your browser's cookie/tracking controls.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with: our payment processor (Razorpay) to complete transactions, our courier/logistics partners to deliver your order, and Meta, in aggregated/hashed form, for ad measurement.

6. Data Retention & Security

We retain order information for as long as necessary to fulfil your order and comply with legal/accounting requirements. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data, though no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data held by us by contacting us using the details below.
