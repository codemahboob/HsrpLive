Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Since HSRP number plates are custom-manufactured specifically for your vehicle's registration number, this policy is designed around that reality. Please read it before ordering.

1. Order Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled free of charge only before manufacturing has started, typically within a few hours of placing the order. Once manufacturing has begun, the order cannot be cancelled as the plate is uniquely made to your entered registration number.

2. Incorrect Details Entered by Customer

We manufacture the plate exactly as the registration number was entered at checkout. If you entered an incorrect number, size, or vehicle type, this is not eligible for a free replacement — a re-manufacturing charge will apply. Please double check all details before completing payment.

3. Damaged, Defective, or Wrong Item Received

If your plate arrives damaged, defective, or different from what you ordered, contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the item and packaging. We will arrange a free replacement or a full refund after verification, at your preference.

4. Failed / Duplicate Payments

If an amount was deducted from your account but the order was not confirmed, it is typically reversed automatically by your bank or Razorpay within 5–7 business days. If it is not reflected after 7 business days, contact us with your payment reference and we will help resolve it.

5. Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method via Razorpay within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank.

6. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Change of mind after manufacturing has started
  • Incorrect registration number, vehicle type, or plate configuration entered by the customer
  • Delivery delays caused by courier, weather, or circumstances beyond our control

7. Contact Us

To request a cancellation, replacement, or refund, call or WhatsApp us at +91 98357 66672.
