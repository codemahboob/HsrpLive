Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Welcome to Custom Plate. As our products are custom-made and personalized, please read this Refund & Cancellation Policy carefully before placing your order.

1. Custom-Made Products

All products sold by Custom Plate are manufactured according to the registration number, name, or custom text provided by the customer. As every order is personalized, it cannot be resold.

2. Order Cancellation

Orders may be cancelled only before production begins. Once production has started, orders cannot be cancelled.

3. Returns

We do not accept returns for personalized products unless:

  • The product received is damaged during transit.
  • The wrong product was delivered.
  • The product has a verified manufacturing defect.

4. Refund Eligibility

A refund or replacement may be approved only if:

  • You contact us within 48 hours of delivery.
  • You provide clear photos or videos showing the issue.
  • The claim is verified by our support team.

5. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Incorrect registration numbers, names, or custom text submitted by the customer.
  • Change of mind after placing the order.
  • Minor variations in embossing, finish, color, spacing, or dimensions resulting from the manufacturing process.
  • Delivery delays caused by courier partners, weather conditions, holidays, or circumstances beyond our control.

6. Refund Process

If your refund request is approved, the refund will be issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days after approval.

7. Replacement Policy

Where applicable, Custom Plate may provide a replacement instead of a refund for damaged, defective, or incorrectly manufactured products.

8. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the refund is due to an error made by Custom Plate.

9. Contact

For any refund or replacement request, please contact us using the contact information available on our website within 48 hours of receiving your order.

Important Notice: By placing an order, you acknowledge that you are purchasing a custom-made personalized product and agree to this Refund & Cancellation Policy before completing your purchase.
